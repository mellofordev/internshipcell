About

The intership cell of SCTCE has been founded by the SCT Students Union with the aim of providing internship oppurtunities for all our students. Most of our students wish to complete interships and earn money by the side. But they don't know where to find good trustable interships and even if they do, they might not have the right skills for the job. The internship cell provides free workshops in collaboration with relevent societies in different fields to upskill yourselves to the level of being a notable candidate for intership among many applications.
